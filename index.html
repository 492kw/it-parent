The problem

Children and teens spend too long on devices

Phones, apps, games, and unrestricted internet access can quickly become a source of conflict, distraction, and unhealthy habits at home.

Parents often lack the technical tools

Many families know what they want to achieve, but not how to configure devices, Wi-Fi, router settings, or parental controls to make it stick.

What IT Parent provides

1. Consulting

Assessment of the household situation, age-appropriate recommendations, and a practical control strategy.

2. Hardware

Recommended and supplied hardware such as routers, network filters, or managed devices that support the plan.

3. Knowledge transfer

Training and documentation so parents can self-manage screen time, internet access, and device rules confidently.

4. Subscription support

Optional ongoing support for updates, troubleshooting, reviews, and help when the family needs to adjust controls as children grow.

Ideal customers

  • Parents wanting healthier device habits at home
  • Families who need stronger screen-time boundaries
  • Households with multiple devices and mixed ages
  • Guardians and carers
  • Parent groups and community networks
  • Families who want to learn, not just outsource
